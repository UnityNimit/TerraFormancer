How TerraFormancer Works

1.

Describe Your Vision

Use the chat to describe the cloud infrastructure you want to build. You can type or use your voice. Be as simple or as complex as you need. For example: "Create an S3 bucket and an EC2 instance."

2.

Review the Output

TerraFormancer will generate the Terraform code and an architecture diagram. Use the tabs on the right to switch between the 'Diagram' and 'Code' views to ensure everything looks correct.

3.

Plan & Deploy

Switch to the 'Deploy' tab. First, click 'Prepare for Deployment' to see the exact plan of changes. After carefully reviewing the plan, click 'Confirm & Apply' to build your infrastructure on AWS.
